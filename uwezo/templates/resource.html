Safety Tips for Dangerous Situations

  • Stay calm and assess the situation quickly
  • Trust your instincts - if something feels wrong, it probably is
  • Be aware of your surroundings at all times
  • Keep your phone charged and easily accessible
  • Use well-lit and populated areas when walking alone
  • Learn basic self-defense techniques
  • Share your location with trusted friends or family
  • Use confident body language to deter potential attackers
  • Know the locations of safe spaces in your area (police stations, hospitals, etc.)
  • If confronted, try to de-escalate the situation verbally before resorting to physical defense
