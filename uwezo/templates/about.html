About Jukumu

Our Mission

Jukumu is dedicated to empowering communities to stand against violence and promote accountability. We believe that by working together, we can create safer, more transparent, and more just societies.

Our platform provides tools for reporting incidents, tracking progress, and fostering community engagement to address issues of violence and accountability in Kenya and beyond.

What We Do

Incident Reporting

Our platform allows users to easily report incidents of violence or corruption, ensuring that no voice goes unheard.

Hotspot Mapping

We provide visual representations of reported incidents, helping to identify areas that need immediate attention and resources.

Progress Tracking

Users can track the progress of reported incidents and see how local authorities and communities are responding to issues.

Community Forum

Our platform hosts a vibrant community forum where users can discuss issues, share experiences, and collaborate on solutions.

Resource Center

We provide a comprehensive resource center with information on legal rights, support services, and educational materials.

Data Analysis

We analyze reported data to identify trends and patterns, helping to inform policy decisions and community actions.
